# Networking

## Overview

The networking portion of this lab is focused on hands-on experience with connectivity, segmentation, service access, and internal routing.

## Areas Worked On

- DHCP reservations
- VLAN configuration
- DNS filtering
- Internal IP management
- Secure remote access
- Tunnel-based service exposure
- Connectivity troubleshooting

## Practical Experience

This lab helped me build practical understanding of how services communicate across a network, how to securely access internal resources remotely, and how to troubleshoot network-related issues when services become unavailable.
